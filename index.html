Why This Isn't Just Another Card Game

WASTA: The Power Play

Influence is currency. Use the Wasta Action Card to cancel opponent actions, secure trades, and bypass conventional rules. It's the ultimate tool for Lebanese-style negotiation.

The Hard Stop at 150

Precision wins. You must hit EXACTLY 150 points to win. Overshoot, and you risk a costly Emergency Dump Trade just to reset your score. Rewards strategy, punishes greed.

Sudden Social Chaos

Cards like Sarsour and Badel (Swap Hands) turn the table into a frenzy of laughter and betrayal. Every turn is high-stakes and highly social.

The Rules in the Souq (Market)

The game uses a rigid, ascending resource chain Hajar, khashab, Akel, Dahab, Kanz and social negotiation.

1. Bank Trade Ratios

The Bank charges a high tax to upgrade your resources in sequence (e.g., Wood $\rightarrow$ Food):

  • **
    • Stone to Wood:Trade 4 Stone for 1 Wood .
  • All Others (Wood, Food, Gold):Trade 3 cards for 1 card of the next tier .
  • Emergency Dump: Special rule to sacrifice Gold/Treasure to the bank to fix an overshot score (over 150 pts).

2. Domestic Trade

Player-to-player trade is fully negotiable (the Souq). This is the only way to downgrade resources (e.g., Food for Wood) and avoid the Bank's high tax.
